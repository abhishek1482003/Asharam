Ahilya sthan

Ahalya Sthan

Ahalya Sthan (also called Ahilya Sthan or Ahilya Asthan) is a Hindu temple located at Ahiyari South in the city of Darbhanga, State of Bihar, India.The temple is dedicated to Ahalya.

Uchchaith durgasthan

Uchchaith durgasthan

Uchchaith is a village in Madhubani District, Bihar, India. There is a temple of the Goddess Durga without head known as Uchchaith Bhagwati in this village. In mythological stories she is known as Banadevi.
